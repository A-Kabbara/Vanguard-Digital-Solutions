FAQ

Questions We Get Asked

How much does it cost?

Every project is scoped individually. We start with a free strategy call to understand your needs, then provide a fixed quote before any work begins.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects launch within 2–4 weeks. A single-service build like an AI receptionist can be live in under a week. Full system builds with website, lead gen, and automation take 3–4 weeks.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. We handle everything technical. You get a clear dashboard and simple instructions for anything you need to manage day-to-day.

Do you work with businesses outside Melbourne?

Yes — we're based in Melbourne but work with businesses across Australia. Everything is delivered remotely with video calls and shared workspaces.

What if I only need one service?

Completely fine. Many clients start with a single service like a website or AI receptionist and add more later. Each service works independently — no lock-in required.

What makes you different from other agencies?

We're AI-first, not AI-as-an-afterthought. Every system we build uses AI to automate the repetitive work that slows your business down. We're also a small, senior team. You work directly with the people building your systems, not account managers.
